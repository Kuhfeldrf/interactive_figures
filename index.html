Figure S1: Protein Distribution Analysis

Stacked bar plots depicting the summed absolute absorbance of peptides grouped by their proteins of origin, highlighting the predominant protein sources and their relative contribution to the peptidomic profile. The top subplot (A and B) display the summed abundance of all peptides whereas bottom subplots (C and D) depicts the summed abundance from exclusively bioactive peptides. The left most subplots (A and C) illustrates the summed abundance of peptides that are mapped to the most prevalent shared proteins 'human and bovine' species. The right most subplots (B and D) summed abundance of peptides that are mapped to either 'bovine', 'human' or 'human and bovine' origins. Only the most abundant proteins are represented in this figure.
